# Unit Economics

## What a converted lead is worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI credits. Assume average deal size of $2,000 in credits at 20% margin = $400 gross profit per customer. With a 12-month LTV assumption and some repurchase rate, a converted customer is worth ~$800-1,200 LTV.

Conservative estimate: **$500 per converted customer.**

## Conversion funnel math

Assume 1,000 audits completed per month:
- 30% capture email = 300 leads
- 10% of leads book a Credex consultation = 30 consultations
- 30% of consultations convert to purchase = 9 customers
- 9 customers × $500 LTV = **$4,500/month revenue**

For $1M ARR: need ~222 customers/month purchasing = ~2,220 consultations/month = ~22,200 leads/month = ~74,000 audits/month.

---

## What would have to be true for $1M ARR in 18 months

1. Tool drives 74,000 audits/month by month 18 — requires strong SEO + viral sharing
2. Email capture rate stays above 25%
3. Consultation booking rate above 8%
4. Sales close rate above 30%
5. Credex builds a sales motion to handle 2,000+ consultations/month

The bottleneck is audit volume. Everything else is a sales and conversion optimization problem.
