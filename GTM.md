# Go To Market

## Exact Target User

Not "startups." Specifically: **Engineering managers or CTOs at 5-20 person B2B SaaS companies** who are responsible for the engineering budget, pay for 3+ AI tools across their team, and have never audited whether they're on the right plans.

They're technical enough to know what Cursor and Claude are. They're senior enough to care about budget. They're too busy to manually compare pricing pages.

## How to get first 100 users in 30 days with $0

1. **Day 1-3:** Post "Show HN: I built a free AI spend auditor for startups" on Hacker News. One well-timed Show HN can drive 500-2000 visitors.

2. **Day 3-7:** Post on r/ExperiencedDevs and r/startups with the angle "I audited our AI spend and found $400/month wasted — built a tool so you can do it in 2 minutes"

3. **Day 7-14:** DM 50 founders on Twitter who have tweeted about AI tool costs. Not a pitch — just "built this, thought you'd find it useful."

4. **Day 14-21:** Submit to Indie Hackers as a product. Write a post: "How I built an AI spend auditor in a week and what I learned."

5. **Day 21-30:** Ask every user who saves >$200/month to share their audit URL on Twitter. The shareable URL is the viral loop.
